# Exploring Dinosaurs

A comprehensive course on dinosaurs designed for individuals who are deaf, blind, or non-verbal, covering the basics of dinosaur biology, evolution, and diversity

## Learning Objectives

1. Understand the definition and characteristics of dinosaurs
2. Learn about the different types of dinosaurs and their classifications
3. Explore the evolution and extinction of dinosaurs
4. Discover the diversity of dinosaur habitats and ecosystems

## Course Sections

1. Introduction to Dinosaurs - This section provides an overview of dinosaur biology, including their definition, characteristics, and importance in the history of life on Earth
