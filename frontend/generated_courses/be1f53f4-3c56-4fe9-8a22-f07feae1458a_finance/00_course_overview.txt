# Introduction to Finance

This comprehensive course covers the fundamentals of finance, including financial planning, investments, and risk management, designed for individuals who are deaf, blind, or non-verbal.

## Learning Objectives

1. Understand the basics of financial planning and budgeting
2. Learn about different types of investments and risk management strategies
3. Develop skills to make informed financial decisions

## Course Sections

1. Foundations of Finance - This section introduces the core concepts of finance, including financial planning, time value of money, and basic financial calculations
